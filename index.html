About

Where craft
meets ceremony.

I'm Muskan — a New York-based event designer and floral artist with a deep love for tactile beauty and considered spaces. Every arrangement, every table, every room I touch is built around the idea that the most meaningful moments deserve surroundings worthy of them.

With an eye trained on texture, proportion, and mood, I create environments that feel both intentional and effortless — the kind of beauty that photographs well but is felt even more deeply in person.

The Process

How we work
together.

01
Discovery

We begin with a conversation — about your vision, your aesthetic, the feeling you want to create. No forms. Just a real exchange.

02
Concept

I translate our conversation into a mood board and design proposal — textures, palette, florals, and spatial flow.

03
Refinement

We refine until every detail is exactly right. Sourcing is handled thoughtfully — local blooms, considered vessels, intentional pairings.

04
Delivery

On the day, I handle everything. You arrive to a space that feels like it was always meant to look this way.
